# Film Adviser — Design Review

---

## 1. Problem Statement

**Original Lab 2 Problem:**
Couples or friends often struggle to decide which movie to watch together.
**Specific User Group:**
Couples or friends who want to pick a movie together
**Specific Action They Cannot Do:**
struggle to select a movie that balances both of their preferences
**Root Cause:**
because their tastes often conflict, streaming services do not track past compromises, and manual negotiation is inefficient
**Concrete Consequence:**
which leads to indecision, frustration, repeated arguments, and wasted time scrolling through endless options
**Final Problem Statement:**
Couples or friends who want to pick a movie together struggle to select a movie that balances both of their preferences because their tastes often conflict, streaming services do not track past compromises, and manual negotiation is inefficient, which leads to indecision, frustration, repeated arguments, and wasted time scrolling through endless options.
